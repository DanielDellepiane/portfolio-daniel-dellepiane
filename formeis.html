/*For me is

Nine people from different countries reflect on love and fear, exploring which past emotion shaped them the most. A heartfelt docu-short inspired by Human film, selected for the First-Time Filmmaker Session 2020.

Nine stories, nine people from different countries share their experience about their way of understanding, seeing and facing two of the emotions that have the greatest influence in our lives: love and fear. And most importantly, which of all emotions felt in the past affected them the most.

The one they still carry with them today and, likely, for the rest of their lives. Each of them with their joys and their restlessness.

A docu-shortfilm that takes the idea from the amazing, awing, project Human, to which I feel very connected to. Candidate at First-time filmmaker session 2020_ Lift-off Global Network.

/*I am Daniel

I was born in Milan, from a Danish father and Italian mother, and lived there until I was 19. In my earlier my formative years, I attended a digital communication-dedicated high school. This meant that from the start, it allowed me to develop little projects and earn something to satisfy some whims, like buying my first bike which I used to ride for 40km to go to school.

A few years ago, I moved to Bologna to study Cinematography at the Alma Mater Studiorum (Università di Bologna). Then, I lived in Santiago de Compostela to study Spanish. Few years ago, I moved to Valencia with the idea of studying a Máster degree in Filmmaking in Barreira & Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia. I lived there for few years and I worked in a Design Studio called Culdesac.

Currently, I am living in A Coruña (Galicia) searching for a new opportunity. The idea of a team and community sharing passion and vision, and working for a purpose is a principle I strongly feel connected to. My main purpose in life is to connect my passions: cinematography, travel, nature and sport into something bigger.
